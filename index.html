ABOUT US

WE ARE THE THIRD GENERATION WORKING IN THE STEEL MANUFACTURING AND TRADING BUSINESS. OUR FIRM WAS FOUNDED IN 1953, AND SINCE THEN, GROUP COMPANIES HAVE EVOLVED INTO A STEEL PROCESSING CENTER WITH FACTORIES IN CHENNAI AND PUNE.

WE ARE AN MSME (GOVERNMENT OF INDIA) REGISTERED UNIT BASED OUT OF AMBATTUR INDUSTRIAL ESTATE, MANUFACTURING COLD DRAWN STEEL BARS AND WIRES, AS WELL AS HEAT TREATED BARS INCLUDING QUENCHED AND TEMPERED STEEL ROUND BARS, STRESS-RELIEVED STEEL BARS, AND PRECISION HEAT-TREATED STEEL BARS WITH SERVICES LIKE ANNEALING, NORMALIZING, HARDENING, AND TEMPERING OF STEEL BARS AND WIRE RODS.

OUR SISTER CONCERN SITUATED ON THE SAME PREMISES IS AN ISO, IATF, NABL ACCREDITED STEEL PROCESSING UNIT SPECIALIZING IN HEAT TREATMENT AND SURFACE COATING OF STEEL BARS AND WIRES, OFFERING PICKLING SERVICES FOR BARS UP TO A MAXIMUM LENGTH OF 6 METERS, AND WIRE ROD COILS WITH A MAXIMUM WEIGHT OF 2.6 MT PER SINGLE COIL. WIRE ROD COIL DIMENSIONS ARE 1250MM OD AND 750MM ID, WITH SERVICES LIKE PHOSPHATE AND LUBE BATH, HEAT TREATMENT OF WIRE COILS, DRAWN WIRES, SPHEROIDIZED ANNEALING, AND SOFT ANNEALING.

FOR WIRE ROD COILS AND WIRES, WE OFFER PICKLING, PHOSPHATE, AND SPHEROIDIZING ANNEALING, SOFT ANNEALING USING 7 MT BELL-TYPE ANNEALING FURNACES, AND COLD DRAWING SERVICES.

Heat Treatments of Steel Bars & Wires.

DIFFERENT TYPES OF HEAT TREATMENTS

THERE ARE SEVERAL TYPES OF HEAT TREATMENT PROCESSES, INCLUDING:

ANNEALING

THIS INVOLVES HEATING STEEL TO A SUITABLE TEMPERATURE AND ALLOWING IT TO COOL OFF VERY SLOWLY UNTIL IT REACHES ROOM TEMPERATURE. IT IS MAINLY A SOFTENING PROCESS DONE TO FACILITATE MACHINING AND COLD WORKING.

NORMALIZING

WHEN STEEL IS NORMALIZED, IT IS HEATED AND HELD AT A PARTICULAR TEMPERATURE FOR A CERTAIN AMOUNT OF TIME DEPENDING ON THE CROSS SECTION BEFORE COOLING IT IN NORMAL AIR.

STRESS RELIEVING

THIS PROCESS INVOLVES HEATING TO A SUITABLE TEMPERATURE, HOLDING LONG ENOUGH TO REDUCE RESIDUAL STRESSES, AND THEN COOLING SLOWLY ENOUGH TO MINIMIZE THE DEVELOPMENT OF NEW RESIDUAL STRESSES.

HARDENING

INCREASING HARDNESS BY SUITABLE TREATMENT USUALLY INVOLVES HEATING AND COOLING TO TRANSFORM THE MICROSTRUCTURE INTO AUSTENITE AT A HIGHER TEMPERATURE AND THEN SUITABLY COOLING BY WATER, OIL, POLYMER, OR AIR TO PRODUCE A HIGH HARDNESS PHASE CALLED MARTENSITE.

SURFACE HARDENING

WITH THIS PROCESS, THE SURFACE OF A PARTICULAR COMPONENT IS HARDENED ENOUGH TO RESIST EROSION AND GENERAL WEAR WHILE MAINTAINING DUCTILITY AND TOUGHNESS TO WITHSTAND SHOCK AND IMPACT LOADING.

TEMPERING

DURING HARDENING, THE MARTENSITE PHASE IS FORMED, WHICH IS A VERY HARD PHASE IN STEEL WITH INSUFFICIENT TOUGHNESS. TEMPERING OF STEELS BY HEATING FOR A CERTAIN TIME BELOW CRITICAL TEMPERATURE IS INTRODUCED TO EXCHANGE SOME OF THE STRENGTH FOR GREATER DUCTILITY.
